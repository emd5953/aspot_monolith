# Smart Scheduling User Guide

## Overview
The Smart Scheduling feature transforms your itinerary generation with intelligent time management, automatic meal planning, and realistic activity scheduling.

## 🎯 Key Features at a Glance

### 1. Activity Density Control
Choose how packed your days should be:

| Density | Activities/Day | Best For |
|---------|---------------|----------|
| 🌴 **Relaxed** | 2-3 activities | Slow travelers, families, leisurely pace |
| ⚖️ **Moderate** | 4-5 activities | Balanced travelers, most people |
| ⚡ **Packed** | 6-8 activities | Energetic travelers, maximizing experiences |

### 2. Automatic Time Scheduling
Every activity gets:
- ⏰ **Start Time** - When to begin
- ⏱️ **End Time** - When it ends
- ⏳ **Duration** - How long it takes

### 3. Smart Meal Planning
Meals automatically inserted at:
- 🥐 **Breakfast**: 8:00 - 10:00 AM
- 🍱 **Lunch**: 12:00 - 2:00 PM
- 🍷 **Dinner**: 6:30 - 8:30 PM

Suggestions based on your cuisine preferences!

### 4. Travel Time Gaps
20-minute buffer between activities for:
- 🚶 Walking between locations
- 🚕 Transportation time
- ☕ Quick breaks

### 5. Interactive Maps
Click any location marker to:
- 📍 See activity details
- 🧭 Get turn-by-turn directions
- 🗺️ Open in Google Maps

## 📱 How to Use

### Step 1: Generate an Itinerary

1. Go to the itinerary generation page
2. Enter your destination
3. Select start and end dates
4. **Choose your activity density:**
   - Click on Relaxed, Moderate, or Packed
   - See the description for each option
5. (Optional) Enable Agentic AI Mode for higher quality
6. Click "Generate Itinerary"

### Step 2: View Your Schedule

Your itinerary now includes:

**List View (Default):**
```
📅 Day 1 - Paris

🥐 French Café & Croissants
   🕐 08:30 - 09:15 (45m)
   📍 Rue de Rivoli, Paris

🏛️ Eiffel Tower
   🕐 09:35 - 11:05 (90m)
   📍 Champ de Mars, 5 Avenue Anatole France

🍱 Trattoria Lunch
   🕐 13:15 - 14:15 (60m)
   📍 Le Marais District
```

**Timeline View:**
- Visual timeline showing activities across the day
- Color-coded by category
- See gaps and overlaps at a glance
- Toggle between List and Timeline views

### Step 3: Navigate to Activities

1. Open the Route Map section
2. See all activities marked on the map
3. Click any marker to see details
4. Click "🧭 Navigate Here" button
5. Google Maps opens with directions!

## 💡 Tips & Best Practices

### Choosing Activity Density

**Choose Relaxed if:**
- ✅ You prefer a slow pace
- ✅ Traveling with young children
- ✅ Want time for spontaneous discoveries
- ✅ Need rest between activities

**Choose Moderate if:**
- ✅ You want a balanced experience
- ✅ First time visiting the destination
- ✅ Mix of activities and downtime
- ✅ Not sure what pace you prefer (default)

**Choose Packed if:**
- ✅ You have limited time
- ✅ High energy and stamina
- ✅ Want to see everything
- ✅ Comfortable with busy schedules

### Making the Most of Timing

1. **Check Start Times**: Plan your morning routine accordingly
2. **Note Meal Times**: Make reservations if needed
3. **Review Gaps**: Use travel time for photos, rest, or shopping
4. **Adjust if Needed**: Use "Edit Day" to modify activities

### Using the Map

1. **Plan Your Route**: See the logical flow of activities
2. **Check Distances**: Ensure locations are reasonable
3. **Save Directions**: Bookmark navigation links
4. **Offline Access**: Screenshot the map for offline use

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Can I change the activity density after generation?**
A: Yes! Use the "Regenerate" button and select a different density.

**Q: What if I don't like the meal suggestions?**
A: You can edit any activity, including meals. Change the title, location, or time.

**Q: Can I add my own activities with custom times?**
A: Yes! Click "Add Activity" and specify your own times.

**Q: Why are there gaps between activities?**
A: 20-minute gaps account for travel time between locations.

**Q: Can I change the day start/end times?**
A: Currently fixed at 9 AM - 11 PM. Custom times coming in future updates!

**Q: Do I need internet for the map?**
A: Yes, for the interactive map. Screenshot it for offline reference.

**Q: Can I export my schedule?**
A: Currently view-only. Export features coming soon!

## 🐛 Troubleshooting

**Activities don't have times:**
- Regenerate the itinerary with the new system
- Old itineraries may not have timing data

**Map markers not showing:**
- Check Google Maps API key is configured
- Ensure location names are specific
- Try regenerating with better location data

**Timeline view not appearing:**
- Ensure activities have start/end times
- Toggle view mode button should appear
- Check that at least one activity has timing

**Navigation button not working:**
- Check popup blockers
- Ensure Google Maps is accessible
- Try opening in a new tab manually
